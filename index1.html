.CASABREEZIA is a Geoportal that presents a map of the Greater Casablanca region containing the following layers: Boundaries of Casablanca communes, Roads, Population, Hotels, Supermarkets, Higher education centers, Background map (satellite images, topo, street,...).

The Geoportal allows citizens to display communes and populations according to several criteria .

The Geoportal allows citizens to search for hotels according to the number of stars and the specified big surfaces.

To interact with us, the geoportal allows you to add complaints and dissatisfactions.

Did you know?

Casablanca - Morocco's largest city and Africa's port!

Casablanca is a city in the nation of Morocco. The city is the country's largest, with a population of 3,359,818 according to the latest census. With such a large population, it's not surprising that it's one of the biggest cities in Africa.
The city has an important inland role, serving as Morocco's main port and also housing a huge financial center. The city is considered a center of economic and commercial activity.
Casablanca covers an area of 220 square kilometers and has a metropolitan population estimated at over 6 million, with a population density of around 27,000 inhabitants per square kilometer.
